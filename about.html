Hello, I am Valerie

I am a productdesigner, graphic designer from Denver Colorado

From the age of eighteen I knew that design was my passion. Since then, for over a decade, I have been professionally active working with business and startups. I specialize in designing and developing user interfaces and digital products. I don't restrict myself to design. The blend of design, user experience, marketing, and business is where I feel I excel.

I speak English, Spanish and Portuguese
